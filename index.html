About Innovation Mahakumbha

MEGA SHOWCASE OF INNOVATION & TALENT

SNDT Women’s University proudly presents - Innovation Mahakumbha – an exhibition of student-led innovative projects from across its departments, conducted and affiliated institutions. This initiative attempts to provide a unique platform to all its stakeholders for creativity, entrepreneurship, grassroots innovation, and academic-industry collaboration in alignment with the vision of a self-reliant India and the objectives of the PM-USHA Scheme.

Over 200 faculty from the University and its affiliated colleges received specialized training in innovation, entrepreneurship, and Agentic AI through the Wadhwani Foundation. This equipped them with advanced skills to drive ideation, problem-solving, and product development.

Guided by these trained faculty, students have built technology-driven prototypes and sustainable solutions. Their entrepreneurial ventures address real-world challenges in healthcare, education, agriculture, energy, digital inclusion, and women’s empowerment.

Teacher & Student Engagement Strategies

200 FDP Trainings aim to expand reach from 50–60 colleges to more, empowering teachers as ambassadors. Class Representatives (CRs) act as student ambassadors to form groups and collect student email IDs for Wadhwani Foundation platform access.

Bootcamps for Innovative Learning

Class Representatives (CRs) facilitate innovation-focused bootcamps by forming student groups and ensuring access to the Wadhwani Foundation platform.

UMIT & NSS Special Force Initiative

Over 10,000 participants will explore innovative projects. Special Bootcamps will focus on Agentic AI + NSS students & teachers, with activities linked to Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
