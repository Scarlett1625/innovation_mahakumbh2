About Innovation Mahakumbha

MEGA SHOWCASE OF INNOVATION & TALENT

SNDT Women‚Äôs University proudly presents - Innovation Mahakumbha ‚Äì an exhibition of student-led innovative projects from across its departments, conducted and affiliated institutions. This initiative attempts to provide a unique platform to all its stakeholders for creativity, entrepreneurship, grassroots innovation, and academic-industry collaboration in alignment with the vision of a self-reliant India and the objectives of the PM-USHA Scheme.

Over 200 faculty from the University and its affiliated colleges received specialized training in innovation, entrepreneurship, and Agentic AI through the Wadhwani Foundation. This equipped them with advanced skills to drive ideation, problem-solving, and product development.
