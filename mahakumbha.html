About Innovation Mahakumbha

A platform for creativity, entrepreneurship, and academic-industry collaboration

Innovation Mahakumbh is an exhibition of student-led innovative projects from across SNDT Women's University departments and affiliated institutions. This initiative provides a unique platform for all stakeholders to showcase creativity, entrepreneurship, grassroots innovation, and academic-industry collaboration.

In collaboration with the Wadhwani Foundation, over 200 faculty from the University's departments and affiliated colleges received specialized training in innovation, entrepreneurship, and Agentic AI. Equipped with advanced skills and tools, they foster a culture of ideation, problem-solving, and product development.

Under their guidance, students have developed technology-driven prototypes, sustainable solutions, and entrepreneurial ventures addressing real-world challenges in healthcare, education, agriculture, energy, digital inclusion, and women's empowerment.
